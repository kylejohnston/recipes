## Ingredients
- **1 Tbsp** olive oil
- **2 Tbsp** bacon dripping (1 Tbsp extra for peas)
- **4 cloves** garlic
- **1/4 to 1/3 cup** Chardonnay
- **9** bacon slices
- **1/3 to 1/2 bag** peas
- **3 eggs** tempered with about 1/3 cup pasta water
- **1 1/4 cups** Parmesan (more for topping)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spaghetti
